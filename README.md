# Houstel.pk — Hostel Management & Accommodation Platform for Pakistan

[Houstel.pk](https://houstel.pk) is Pakistan's all-in-one hostel platform — 
combining a **student accommodation booking system** with a full 
**hostel management solution** for hostel owners.

## 🏠 For Students & Tenants

Find and book verified hostels across Pakistan:

- 🔍 Search hostels by city, price, and amenities
- 📋 View room details, availability, and photos
- 💳 View payment history and dues
- 🛏️ Track your booking and room assignment
- 👤 Personal student portal

## 🏢 For Hostel Owners & Managers

A complete hostel management system built for Pakistan:

- 👥 Manage tenants / students (add, edit, remove)
- 💰 Payment management & tracking (rent, dues, receipts)
- 🍽️ Mess menu management (weekly/monthly meal plans)
- 🛏️ Room & bed assignment management
- 📊 Dashboard with occupancy and revenue overview
- 📣 Announcements & notifications for students
