Houstel.pk — Pakistan's Hostel Management & Accommodation Platform

Houstel.pk is Pakistan's all-in-one hostel solution — a complete hostel management system combined with a student accommodation booking platform. Whether you are a student looking for affordable housing or a hostel owner wanting to manage your property digitally, Houstel.pk has you covered.

🏠 For Students & Tenants

  • Search and book verified hostels across Pakistan
  • Personal student portal to manage your stay
  • View room details, bed assignment and availability
  • Track payments, dues and receipts online
  • View booking history and current status
  • Access mess menu and meal schedules

🏢 For Hostel Owners & Managers

  • Complete hostel management dashboard
  • Manage tenants — add, edit, and remove students
  • Payment management — track rent, dues and generate receipts
  • Mess menu management — plan weekly and monthly meals
  • Room and bed assignment management
  • Announcements and notifications for students
  • Occupancy and revenue overview reports
  • Customer management with full tenant profiles
